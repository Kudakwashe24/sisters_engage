More About Sisters Engage

Sisters Engage is a movement that fosters women empowerment

  • - Our core purpose is to advocate sisterly love among women.
  • - To encourage women to speak up and find healing they deserve.
  • - To help women get the help they need e.g. therapy, mentors and psycologist.
  • - To push women to better themselves in all aspects of life e.g. mentally, financially and physically.
